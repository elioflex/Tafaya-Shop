# Admin Guide - How to Add Products to Tafaya Shop

## Accessing the Admin Panel

1. **Open your browser** and go to: **http://localhost:3000/admin**
2. You'll see the Admin Panel with a list of all products

## Adding a New Product

### Step 1: Click "Add New Product"
- Look for the blue button at the top that says **"+ Add New Product"**
- Click it to open the product form

### Step 2: Fill in Product Details

#### **Product Name** (Required)
- Enter the name of your ashtray
- Example: "Classic Ceramic Ashtray" or "Handmade Tfaya Skull Design"

#### **Description** (Required)
- Describe your product in detail
- Example: "Beautiful handmade ceramic ashtray with intricate Moroccan patterns. Each piece is unique and crafted with care."

#### **Price (MAD)** (Optional)
- Enter the price in Moroccan Dirhams
- Example: 150 or 200
- Leave empty if you prefer to discuss pricing via WhatsApp

#### **Product Image**
You have TWO options:

**Option A: Upload an Image**
- Click the "Upload Image" button
- Select an image from your computer
- You'll see a preview of the image

**Option B: Use an Image URL**
- If you have an image hosted online, paste the URL in the text field
- Example: https://example.com/my-ashtray.jpg

### Step 3: Save the Product
- Click the **"Add Product"** button
- Your product will appear in the products list immediately!

## Editing a Product

1. Find the product in the list
2. Click the **blue pencil icon** (Edit button)
3. Modify any details you want
4. Click **"Update Product"**

## Deleting a Product

1. Find the product in the list
2. Click the **red trash icon** (Delete button)
3. Confirm the deletion
4. Product will be removed immediately

## Tips

- **Image Size**: Keep images under 5MB for best performance
- **Image Format**: Use JPG, PNG, or WebP formats
- **Descriptions**: Be detailed! Customers love to know about the craftsmanship
- **Pricing**: You can leave price empty and discuss it via WhatsApp with customers

## Viewing Your Shop

- Click **"View Shop"** button in the top right to see how customers see your products
- Or visit: http://localhost:3000
